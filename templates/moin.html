そんな: Such, that kind of
ヒョロヒョロに: Wimpy, weakly (ヒョロヒョロ = onomatopoeia for being weak/thin, に = adverbial particle)
何なにが: What ( 何なに = what, が = subject marker)
できる: Can do (potential form of する)
の: Question marker, explanatory
さ: Emphatic particle
